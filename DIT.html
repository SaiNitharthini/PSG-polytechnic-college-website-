About Department

The Department of Information Technology was established in the year 2006 with a view to develop quality engineers to meet the current trends in the emerging world of IT.

Vision

Department of Information Technology aspires to be a pioneer in the field of Information Technology to provide technological support to the industries towards their growth and development of the society.

Mission

The mission of Department of Information Technology is to provide curriculum that facilitate student centered and laboratory based learning on information technologies that are:

  • M1: Relevant to their lives and career
  • M2: Valuable in terms of content and competencies
  • M3: Connected to the needs of industry

Course Structure

CodeDescription
PO1Basic and Discipline Specific Knowledge: Apply knowledge of basic mathematics, science and engineering fundamentals to solve engineering problems.
PO2Problem Analysis: Identify and analyse well-defined engineering problems using codified standard methods.
PO3Design / Development of Solutions: Design solutions for well-defined technical problems and systems components.
PO4Engineering Tools, Experimentation and Testing: Apply modern engineering tools and appropriate techniques.
PO5Engineering Practices for Society: Apply appropriate technology in context of society, sustainability, and ethics.
PO6Project Management: Use engineering management principles individually or as a team member.
PO7Life-long Learning: Ability to analyse individual needs and engage in updating in technological changes.
                               
CodeDescription
PSO1Acquire detailed knowledge on the current trends and issues in IT and develop skills in new programming environments.
PSO2Analyze, design and develop software systems applying current technical concepts in information management and networking.
                                       
CodeDescription
PEO1Excel as an IT professional with proficiency in understanding and designing solutions to real time challenges.
PEO2Have the ability to function effectively on teams to accomplish a common goal.
PEO3Aware of the social responsibilities in building the society.
