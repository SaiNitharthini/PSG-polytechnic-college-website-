Department of Chemistry

Inculcating Scientific Discipline | Since 1939

About Us

The Department of Chemistry was established in the year 1939 and it actively supports the Engineering departments by handling Chemistry subject for all the diploma courses. The faculty of the Department is actively involved in teaching, consultancy work and research. The students are given training in analytical methods and are taught about the need and application of engineering materials. The main focus on laboratory training is to inculcate a sense of discipline. Depending on the need the students of the diploma in Foundry Technology are given a specialize training in metallurgical analysis supported by a sound theoretical background. Further the department extends facilities and guidance to final year diploma students who take up interdisciplinary and industry oriented projects.

Vision

To be a forerunner to equip the students with the fundamental knowledge on Chemical Science to understand the mechanical, chemical and metallurgical properties of Engineering Materials.

Mission

  • The basic chemistry and chemical reactions
  • Interpretation to specific behavior of different Engineering materials
  • Practical applications of chemistry in selection and use of materials for different Engineering requirements

Facilities

CHEMISTRY LABORATORY

The Chemistry Laboratory is spread over 1860 sq.ft. and can accommodate 40 students at a time. It is equipped with facilities for Physical, Analytical and Inorganic Chemistry experiments. The laboratory includes LPG gas pipeline systems, de-ionized water supply, fume cupboards, exhaust systems and modern analytical instruments to support practical training, research and consultancy activities.
