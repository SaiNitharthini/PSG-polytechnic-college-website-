In Pace with Technology, Industrial Standards and Real Time Needs

About Department

Since 2006, the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering at PSG Polytechnic College has been progressing towards excellence in the field of teaching, placement and career guidance. Curriculum helps to keep in pace with technology, industrial standards and real time industrial needs.

MODROB: AICTE has sanctioned a total sum of Rs. 16, 75, 345/- to set up Smart Electronics Lab under Modernization and Removal of Obsolescence Polytechnic (MODROB-POL) Scheme.

Vision

The department strives to develop skilled technical professionals who are competent to cater to the ever-changing industrial demand and societal needs.

Mission

The mission of the department is to facilitate young diploma engineers to:

  • M1: Acquire technical knowledge and skills in the field of Electronics and Communication Engineering.
  • M2: Face and solve the challenges in the field of ECE and attain successful career in Industry/academics.
  • M3: Serve for the well being of society addressing their needs.

Course Structure

CodeDescription
PO1Basic and Discipline specific knowledge: Apply knowledge of basic mathematics, science and engineering fundamentals and engineering specialization to solve the engineering problems.
PO2Problem analysis: Identify and analyse well-defined engineering problems using codified standard methods.
PO3Design/ development of solutions: Design solutions for well-defined technical problems and assist with the design of systems components or processes to meet specified needs.
PO4Engineering Tools, Experimentation and Testing: Apply modern engineering tools and appropriate technique to conduct standard tests and measurements.
PO5Engineering practices for society, sustainability and environment: Apply appropriate technology in context of society, sustainability, environment and ethical practices.
PO6Project Management: Use engineering management principles individually, as a team member or a leader to manage projects and effectively communicate about well-defined engineering activities.
PO7Life-long learning: Ability to analyse individual needs and engage in updating in the context of technological changes.
CodeDescription
PSO1To apply the concepts learnt in the field of electronics and communication domain to build application oriented engineering systems
PSO2To apply technical skills to solve challenging societal tasks in ethical and economical manner
CodeDescription
PEO1Apply the acquired knowledge to provide innovative solutions to challenging technological advancements in the discipline
PEO2Exhibit problem solving and lifelong learning skills and adapt to emerging trends by continuous learning
PEO3Accomplish career success by practicing professional integrity and be socially responsible by contributing to enhance the quality of life

Facilities

Electronics Laboratory (I-402)

Equipped with measuring instruments and trainer kits for PCB Assembling, Digital Circuits, and Consumer Electronics courses. Inaugurated by Principal Dr. B. Giriraj.

Communication Systems Laboratory (I-404)

Used for designing oscillators, filters and operational amplifiers. Supports Electronic Devices, LIC and Communication Engineering courses.

Computer Laboratory (I-409)

Equipped with hardware and software for Embedded Systems, Mobile Communication, VLSI and Microcontroller Interfacing.

Product Development Centre (PDC)

Established in 2018 for Research & Development activities. Provides facilities for prototype development, patent work and XILINX FPGA training.
