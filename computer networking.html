About Department

The Department of Computer Networking was started in the year 1998 and accredited by NBA in the year 2011. This Department offers 3 years Diploma Programme in Computer Networking. The Department is well equipped with separate Computer Hardware, Computer Networking and Wireless Networks Laboratories with experienced faculty members. Text books, References and Journals are present in the department library for the reference of students and faculty members. The department organizes various continuing education Programmes for students and Industrialists.

Vision

The Department aspires to be a frontier in the area of Computer Networking and be recognized as a leading source of sculpting most promising Network Professionals and Entrepreneurs for Industries and Society.

Mission

  • M1: To acquire technical exposure in the area of Computer Networking
  • M2: To administrate, manage the latest techniques in the field of Computer Networking
  • M3: To hone their skills to compete in the global market pace.

Facilities

COMPUTER NETWORKING LAB

This laboratory provides the students hands-on experience on installing and configuring networks, network operating systems and is highly equipped with modernized equipment funded through MODROBS with network devices like Firewall and Router. This enhancement helps in imparting the latest practical skills in Network Security, Installing NOS and integration of different network topologies using Cisco and Microsoft technologies.

PSG-ETS IoT LABORATORY

PSG- ETS IoT Laboratory is associated with Industry M/s Enthu technology solutions India Pvt Ltd. They sponsored cloud services worth Rupees Ninety Thousand for IoT data services and APIs. Internet of things is the future of connectivity. This laboratory provides the students hands-on experience in Internet of Things (IoT) is the network of physical objects or 'things' embedded with electronics, software, sensors, and connectivity to enable objects to exchange data. IoT allows direct integration between the physical world and computer-based systems, helping to connect people, processes and devices.

WIRELESS NETWORKS LAB

This lab is equipped to cater to the needs of students to execute experiments on wireless networks. The Laboratories provide facilities for the students to acquire more knowledge on wireless devices to understand different technologies involved in wireless communication. The laboratory can also be used for air protocols like GSM, CDMA etc. wireless LAN, Bluetooth, client side and network side Wireless Equipments.

AR/VR LAB

This lab provides students to explore the cutting – edge technologies of Augmented Reality (AR) & Virtual Reality (VR) and their applications across various fields. It fosters innovation, encourages hands-on learning AR&VR, and helps bridge the gap between the digital virtual world and physical worlds. The technologies and tools used are Unity 3D with VR/AR plugins, and hardware like VR headsets, software like Blender (3D modelling), Adobe Suite, etc. This lab plays an integral role in preparing the students to shape the future of these transformative technologies.

AI LAB

AI lab provides a hands-on learning environment where students can explore the possibilities of AI and develop critical thinking, problem-solving, and coding skills. This lab is dedicated to exploring and developing technologies that allow machines to think, learn, and perform tasks that would normally require human intelligence. In an AI lab, students create and improve technologies that can understand speech, recognize images, make decisions, and even play games, and also introduce students to the exciting field of AI and inspire them to think about how AI can be used to solve real-world problems. Additionally, AI lab prepare students for future careers in technology, engineering, and research, which are some of the fastest-growing fields like Machine Learning, NLP(Natural Language Processing), Computer vision and Robotics.
