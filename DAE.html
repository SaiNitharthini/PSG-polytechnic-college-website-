About Department

Department of Automobile Engineering was started in the year 2004. With a good team of faculty members and young Alumni support, the department is progressing towards excellence in the field of teaching and placement in various companies. The department focuses on creating skilled professionals who can supplement the needs and demands of the rapidly evolving automotive industry and society.

Vision

Department of Automobile Engineering aspires to be a centre of excellence for creating skilled and talented professionals who supplement the needs and demands of the industry and society.

Mission

The mission of the department is to create opportunities for the students to fine-tune their interest and talent in Automobile Engineering through:

  • Exposure to current technological development.
  • Involvement in fostering industry institute interaction.
  • Developing technical skill and confidence to become an entrepreneur.

Course Structure

CodeDescription
PO1Basic and Discipline specific knowledge: Apply knowledge of basic mathematics, science and engineering fundamentals and engineering specialization to solve the engineering problems.
PO2Problem analysis: Identify and analyse well-defined engineering problems using codified standard methods.
PO3Design/ development of solutions: Design solutions for well-defined technical problems and assist with the design of systems components or processes to meet specified needs.
PO4Engineering Tools, Experimentation and Testing: Apply modern engineering tools and appropriate technique to conduct standard tests and measurements.
PO5Engineering practices for society: Apply appropriate technology in context of society, sustainability, environment and ethical practices.
PO6Project Management: Use engineering management principles individually or as a team to manage projects and communicate activities.
PO7Life-long learning: Ability to analyse individual needs and engage in updating in the context of technological changes.
CodeDescription
PSO1Apply the knowledge of manufacturing, assembly and testing in automotive industry
PSO2Adapt to work in an automotive industry as service and maintenance engineer.
CodeDescription
PEO1Pursue a successful career in the field of automobile engineering or a relevant field
PEO2Be able to develop new innovative solutions for problems faced in the industry by continuously updating their knowledge about latest technologies
PEO3Be able to work effectively in multi-disciplinary and multi-cultural teams contributing to the needs of the industry and society at large.

Facilities

Automobile Chassis & Transmission Lab

Comprises of all types of transmission systems like clutch assembly, gear box assembly and differential unit for hands-on learning.

Automobile Engines Lab

Students perform testing, troubleshooting, overhauling, dismantling and assembling process of various engines.

Thermal Engineering Lab

Equipped with Petrol & Diesel engines (2 & 4 stroke), Refrigeration & Air Conditioning tutors, Morse Test setup, and Air Compressor rigs.

Automobile Electrical and Electronics Lab

Focuses on developing skills in electrical and electronic devices like starter motors, battery systems, alternators, and modern automotive sensors.

Two Wheeler Servicing Laboratory

Provides practical training in the inspection, adjustment, and servicing of major components specifically for two-wheelers.
