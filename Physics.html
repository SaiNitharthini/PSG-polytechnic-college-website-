Department of Physics

Imparting Physical Science Excellence | Since 1939

About Us

Department of Physics was established in the year 1939. The department has been working towards fulfilling its mission through teaching the basic concepts of Physics to first year diploma students. Faculty members of the department are actively involved in teaching, research and consultancy work. The syllabi impart both technical and practical knowledge to the students in the field of Physics by focusing the content of the syllabus towards electrical, mechanical and textile streams. The students are given training in analytical methods and are taught about the concepts which will be useful for their further studies.

Vision

The vision of the Department of Physics is to be an excellence in the field of physical science wherein the students are made competent to understand the fundamental concepts in technical education and foster their skill development.

Mission

  • To understand the basic concepts of science in technical applications
  • To apply the acquired technical knowledge in industries
  • To motivate the students towards career development

  • Viscosity of high and low viscous liquids
