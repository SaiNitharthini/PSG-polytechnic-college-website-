About Department

The Department of Apparel Technology was established in the year 1999, to facilitate the young Diploma Apparel Technocrats to acquire the technical expertise in the field of Apparel Technology, enabling them to manage the existing & developing technologies and acquire the skills necessary to face and manage the challenges emerging in the industry. It is also intended to infuse creativeness and self-confidence to handle the affairs of the industry and to hone their skills to compete in the emerging globalization scenario.

Vision

The Department facilitates to be the pioneer in the field of apparel and fashion technology for enabling the students to acquire the creativeness and technical expertise and to become a promising Apparel technologist, Designers and Entrepreneurs for being responsible citizen in the global community.

Mission

  • To infuse the creativity and technical knowledge in the area of apparel and fashion technology
  • To administrate, manage the latest industry techniques and practices in the field of apparel technology
  • To acquire career skill set needed in apparel workplace for facing technological and managerial demands of the global apparel industry.

Course Structure

ComponentOutcome
PO1Basic and Discipline specific knowledge: Apply knowledge of basic mathematics, science and engineering fundamentals and engineering specialization to solve the engineering problems.
PO2Problem analysis: Identify and analyze well defined engineering problems using codified standard methods.
PO3Design / development of solutions: Design solutions for well-defined technical problems and assist with the design of systems components or processes to meet specified needs.
PO4Engineering Tools, Experimentation and Testing: Apply modern engineering tools and appropriate technique to conduct standard tests and measurements.
PO5Engineering practices for society, sustainability and environment: Apply appropriate technology in context of society, sustainability, environment and ethical practices.
PO6Project Management: Use engineering management principles individually, as a team member or a leader to manage projects and effectively communicate about well-defined engineering activities.
PO7Life-long learning: Ability to analyse individual needs and engage in updating in the context of technological changes.
PSO NoProgramme Specific Outcomes
PSO1Ability to apply apparel production techniques, pattern engineering and garment construction skills for manufacturing quality apparel products.
PSO2Expertise in fashion designing, apparel CAD, merchandising and quality evaluation to meet global industry standards.

Facilities

PATTERN MAKING AND GRADING LABORATORY

This laboratory is equipped with the collection of dress forms, Mannequins and cork top tables. The students are trained in this laboratory to develop flat patterns and grading for various styles of garment of knitted and woven.

GARMENT CONSTRUCTION LABORATORY

The laboratory is well equipped with modern heavy duty sewing machines like High Speed Lock Stitch, Overlock, Flat Lock, Button Hole, Button Stitch and Utility Decorative Stitch sewing machines.

APPAREL MACHINERY AND EQUIPMENT LABORATORY

This laboratory is equipped with latest heavy duty sewing machines. Students are given hands-on training on various machines to understand their parts and functions.

APPAREL CAD LABORATORY

The Apparel CAD Laboratory is equipped with Orbito CAD software for pattern making, grading, marker planning and cut planning.

CHEMICAL PROCESSING LABORATORY

The Textile Chemical Processing Laboratory comprises a wide range of equipment for processing fibres, yarns, fabrics and garments.

TEXTILE TESTING LABORATORY

The laboratory is equipped with modern textile testing instruments for testing fibres, yarns, fabrics and garments according to industrial standards.
