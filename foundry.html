About Department

The Department of Foundry Technology was established in the year 1996 to cater the manpower and R & D development needs of local foundries. The need for specialized foundry technicians was felt in the late 1990s when the small and medium foundries in Coimbatore started growing rapidly.

Vision

Department of Foundry Technology aspires to accomplish national recognition in the field of foundry and materials engineering by imparting technical skills to students and industries towards development of society.

Mission

The mission of the Department of Foundry Technology is to expose students to industrial requirements by:

  • Curriculum development fostering industrial needs.
  • Encouraging students to be entrepreneurs.
  • Undertaking industrial projects.

Course Structure

PO1Basic and Discipline specific knowledge: Apply knowledge of basic mathematics, science and engineering fundamentals and engineering specialization to solve the engineering problems.
PO2Problem analysis: Identify and analyse well-defined engineering problems using codified standard methods.
PO3Design / development of solutions: Design solutions for well-defined technical problems and assist with the design of systems components or processes to meet specified needs.

PO5Engineering practices for society, sustainability and environment: Apply appropriate technology in context of society, sustainability, environment and ethical practices.

Facilities

Strength of Materials Laboratory
Laboratory facilities are available for testing material strength and related mechanical properties required for engineering applications.
Sand Testing Laboratory
Foundry sand testing laboratory is equipped with necessary equipment to conduct tests on foundry sand properties. Major equipment includes tensile testing machine, flowability test setup, AFS test, shatter index and permeability test.
Foundry Simulation Laboratory
This lab houses 35 networked computers with systems configured for modelling and simulation. Software includes Pro-Engineer (V5.0), AutoCAD 2014 and casting simulation tools to simulate foundry processes.
Metrology Laboratory
The Metrology lab is equipped with various instruments, gauges and measuring machines. Major equipment includes Optical Profile Projector, Tool Makers Microscope, Surface Roughness Testers, Concentricity Tester and Mechanical Comparator.
Metallography Laboratory
This laboratory contains the equipment needed for viewing microstructural phases present in metals and alloys using a metallurgical microscope. It also supports heat treatment processes for microstructural modification using a muffle furnace.
