About Us

The Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering was started in the year 1939. The Department maintains good rapport with industries. This Department offers three year Diploma Programme in Electrical and Electronics Engineering which was accredited by NBA in 2005 and 2011 and four year Diploma Programme in Electrical and Electronics Engineering (Sandwich). The Department is equipped with laboratories for teaching both basic and applied courses in Electrical and Electronics Engineering like Electrical Machines, Power Electronics, Microprocessor and Microcontroller, Computer Simulation, Analog and Digital Electronics.

Apart from the curriculum, experts are invited for specialised topics to add additional skills for the students. Guest lectures are arranged through associations for the students in order to be in-line with the industries. Students are visiting reputed industries during their course of study. Students are provided opportunities to undergo internship, Inplant training in PSG Industrial Institute. This Department offers well equipped faculty members.

Vision

Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering aspires to be a recognized centre for imparting the state of the art knowledge in the field of Electrical and Electronics Engineering for the development of the society.

Mission

The mission of Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering is to impart application oriented technical education to

• Develop students to meet industry requirements
• Evolve innovative applications of technology
• Create career advancement opportunities
• Disseminate the knowledge and skills acquired

Course Structure

PODescription
POBasic and Discipline specific knowledge : Apply knowledge of basic mathematics, science and engineering fundamentals and engineering specialization to solve the engineering problems.
POProblem analysis: Identify and analyse well-defined engineering problems using codified standard methods.
PODesign/ development of solutions: Design solutions for well-defined technical problems and assist with the design of systems components or processes to meet specified needs.
POEngineering Tools, Experimentation and Testing: Apply modern Engineering tools and appropriate technique to conduct standard tests and Measurements.
POpractices for society, sustainability and environment: Apply technology context in context of society, sustainability, environment and ethical practices.
POProject Management: Use engineering management principles individually,as a team member or a leader to manage projects and effectively communicate about well-defined engineering activities.
POLife-long learning: Ability to analyse individual needs and engage in updating in the context of technological changes
PSODescription
PSO1Apply principles of Electrical and Electronics Engineering for constructing, operating, testing and maintenance of Electrical and Electronics systems
PSO2Students will be able to work and implement the practical skill set in Electronics, Electrical machines, Power systems, Measurements and Instrumentation and Embedded systems.
PEODescription
PEOProvide strong technical foundation and practical skills in the field of Electrical and Electronics Engineering to meet the demand of industry.
PEO Empower with creative skill set to implement, analyze and troubleshoot problems in Electrical and Electronics field.
PEO Emphasis with emerging technologies, to innovate, enhances ethics, lifelong learning and provide solution for the improvement

PSG - Fuji Centre of Excellence in Energy and Automation

Inauguration

PSG - Fuji Centre of Excellence in Energy and Automation, established in the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering at PSG Polytechnic College. Fuji Electric India Pvt. Ltd. has sponsored equipment under CSR initiative. The new centre of excellence (CoE) is armed with Programmable Logic Controller (PLCs), Human Machine Interface (HMI) and various modern control electric drives to enhance the knowledge and skills of students in Energy and Automation.

It aims to bridge the gap between academic learning and industrial requirements by providing advanced training facilities in industrial automation, energy management, and control systems. Through this initiative, students gain hands-on experience with modern industrial equipment. The company also supports skill development by conducting workshops, technical training programs and faculty development sessions. This collaboration helps students to improve their practical knowledge, develop industry-relevant skills and enhance their employability in the field of Electrical and Automation Engineering.

PSG-Schneider Electrician Training Centre Inauguration

PSG Polytechnic College, Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering along with Schneider Electric India, Bengaluru jointly established "PSG-Schneider Electrician Training Centre" at PSG Polytechnic College. This lab is sponsored by Schneider India Foundation Limited, Bengaluru. Laboratory is equipped with all Electrical Wiring and demo boards with necessary accessories and tools. This centre will be utilised by the students of EEE and allied branches, PSG Polytechnic College, PSG Industrial Institute and Skill Training Students.

An objective of the partnership with Schneider Electric India Foundation and PSG PTC is to train the students for the need of the industry. Skillful training will be provided to gain more practical knowledge in Electrical wiring from well trained faculty members from Schneider and PSG Polytechnic College with an enriched equipment in the centre. Trainees who are school or college drop outs would be made job ready in just 3 – 4 months of holistic training.
