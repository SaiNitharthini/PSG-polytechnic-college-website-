English Department

Nurturing Communication and Professional Excellence

About the Department

The department of English has been functioning as a fully fledged department for the past seven decades, and is equipped with qualified and dedicated team of faculty sharing their expertise in various fields of English language teaching and curriculum development. Further it facilitates the students to hone their soft skills and exhibit their linguistic abilities to meet the expectations of the industries. They have been given exposure to Group Discussions, oral presentations and mock interviews. In addition to these professional skills, students are given adequate practices in professional writing.

Vision

Department of English envisions enhancement of a fully fledged language laboratory with an audio visual studio and conduct of regular training programmes focusing on Spoken English, Technical Communication and Excellence in Business Communication.

Mission

  • To provide the students with necessary language skills to succeed at the professional arena.
  • To facilitate them to hone their communication skills
  • To motivate them to become confident individuals to meet the societal challenges

Activities & Facilities

Placement training classes are conducted for the final year placement enrolled students on alternate working Saturdays. Major topics covered:

  • Self Introduction
  • Group Discussion
  • Resume Writing
  • How to answer questions in an interview

On the final week, students undergo a Mock Interview with a panel consisting of Engineering and English department faculty.

Best Educator Award: Mr. I. Gogul, Lecturer, Department of English, received the Best Educator Award from PEARL Foundation Educational Excellence Awards on 10th December 2017.

The Communication Laboratory is designed to enhance and enrich the students' knowledge of English. It serves as a hub for developing proper linguistic skills, ensuring ease of communication in their future professional careers.
