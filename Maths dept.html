About Us

The Department was established in the year 1939. It actively supports the Engineering Departments by handling Mathematics and Statistics for all the Diploma Courses. The curriculum and syllabi are updated to cater to the needs of industries and also to equip the students in further studies. The Department consists of dedicated faculty members and is known for its discipline and concept-oriented subject knowledge through high-quality teaching.

Vision

The vision of the Department of Mathematics of PSG Polytechnic College is to develop as a model centre for imparting Mathematical problem solving skills among students towards their career development.

Mission

  • Imparting Mathematical knowledge
  • Practical applications of Mathematics of Engineering problems
  • Promoting the career development interest among students
